A beautiful paradox.

The many contrasts of the new Flying Spur create a journey of constant discovery.
Its intricate details are based on a century of Bentley craftsmanship – as well as cutting-edge modern techniques.
Its technology is always innovative – but it is so intuitive to use, it never interrupts your enjoyment of the drive.
Its breathtaking performance begins with immense power – and yet it possesses a remarkable, almost delicate, agility.
The new Flying Spur is whatever you want it to be, at any given moment.

design

A grand design. With a wealth of intricate details.

A DRAMATIC EXTERIOR

With an assertive, modern design, the new Flying Spur’s sharp lines, sculpted form and balanced proportions combine to create a commanding presence. Its considered details, including a new vertical vane matrix grille, cut-crystal effect headlamps, a 21” twin ten-spoke bright painted wheel as standard, and an optional new illuminating Flying B mascot, create a car with charisma and personality.
The discovery of each new detail takes you on an inspiring journey.

A HARMONIOUS INTERIOR

The cabin of the new Flying Spur takes craftsmanship to new levels.
Its contemporary design, with an emphasis on comfort and refinement, creates a stylish and relaxing environment for both the driver and those who prefer to be driven.

The harmonious interior features a new twin flute seat design – or if you ptfor the Mulliner Driving Specification, you can select the new Lofted Diamond design that combines intricate stitching and embroidery.
The Crown Cut Walnut veneer, with its horizontal straight-cut grain, contributes to a feeling of width and spaciousness. Sculpted vents in a polished or optional diamond-knurled finish add to the refined feel, while the panoramic glass sunroof lets light flood into the cabin.

Curated details, for you to handpick

Every attribute of the new Flying Spur has been considered from every angle by the expert designers and engineers at Crewe. Each aspect combines the finest materials with skilled craftsmanship to create something truly captivating.
When you commission your car, you can make those details even more special by choosing the ones that resonate with you.

EXTERIOR FLAIR

Part of the distinctive beauty of the new Flying Spur lies in making it yours. Starting with a palette of 17 standard colours, carefully chosen to complement the exterior style, there is the option to choose from the extended range, giving you a total of 30 colours.

Two exclusive new 21” ten twin spoke alloy wheels are available in a choice of finishes. The Mulliner Driving Specification also gives you the option of a 22” wheel, available in a choice of two finishes to match the balanced proportions and unique character of the new Flying Spur.
